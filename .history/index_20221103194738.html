Aquamarine
(Be₃Al₂SiO₆)

Elemental Properties

  • Oxygen: 8 protons, gas
  • Aluminum: 13 protons, metal
  • Silicon: 14 protons, metalloid
  • Beryllium: 4 protons, metal

Electron Configuration

  • Oxygen: [He] 2s2 2p4
  • Aluminum: [Ne] 3s2 3p1
  • Silicon: [Ne] 3s2 3p2
  • Beryllium: [He] 2s2

Is the Molecule Aquamarine Polar?

The substance aquamarine is considered ionic because it is made up of metals bonded with non metals and metals donate valence electrons to the non metals.
